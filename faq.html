Frequently Asked Questions

What is this tool?

CloudCheckr is a tool brought to you by CloudCap. It's a static analysis tool for your cloud infrastructure. Think of it as a spell checker, but for costs. You upload your CloudFormation file and your AWS Cost Estimation file, then we do the math and tell you if your cost estimation is in the ballpark.

Why is it free? What's the catch?

Right now, CloudCap is in alpha, so we are putting CloudCheckr out in the wild. All we ask is for you to provide any feedback you have on the tool for the next iteration of the project.

Where does my data go?

We understand that security and privacy are important, especially for cloud applications and infrastructure. Currently, we do not keep or store any of your data (CloudFormation file or Cost Estimation file). We simply pipe your file through our tool and deliver the results to you.

Who are you? Why should I trust you?

We are a group of researchers based out of Columbia University in New York, dedicated to bringing cutting-edge research into industry use. For more information on who we are, visit our LinkedIn page or follow us on Twitter.
